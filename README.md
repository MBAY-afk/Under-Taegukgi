Please run this using Chrome. The initial screen shows a waving Korean flag and the text "Click to Start." The flag animation may experience some lag. If the screen is black and only shows "Click to Start," please wait a moment. If the background still does not appear, try refreshing the page. If it still doesn't show up, please reopen the page in Chrome.

After entering, a video will play accompanied by text. You will then arrive at the main interface, which features a world map. Use the scroll wheel to zoom in and out, and drag the mouse to pan across the map. Foreign policies are displayed on the map and require a significant amount of National Executive Power to implement. National Executive Power is represented by the yellow bar at the bottom and is a valuable resource.

The top-left corner displays the National Status, including Technology, Economy, Industry, and Stability, as well as the speed of time progression, which you can control manually. The top-right corner features the President. Domestic Policies are located in the bottom-left corner; click to spend National Executive Power to implement them. The bottom-right corner contains National Industrial Development Policies, which can be researched for free and provide immediate benefits upon completion. World News events will affect the National Status.
